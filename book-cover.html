Dasia Williams

Romance Book Cover

For this project, I meticulously crafted three captivating book covers and jackets for contemporary romance novels, each intricately woven around the theme of the "red thread of fate." Drawing inspiration from the theory that soulmates are invisibly bound by this symbolic thread, I employed innovative techniques.
Emphasizing the nature of the concept, I incorporated the actual embroidery of a red string onto each cover. Through thoughtful experimentation with artistic techniques, I succeeded in visually expressing the invisible yet profound connection suggested by the theory. The result is a set of three cohesive covers that not only tell a story but beautifully capture the essence of the "red thread of fate" storyline behind each novel.
Made using Procreate, Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, and embroidery thread
